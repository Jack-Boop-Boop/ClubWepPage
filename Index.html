Purpose

The Computer Science Society exists to build a strong, inclusive community for students passionate about technology. We aim to encourage collaboration, curiosity, and growth through workshops, guest speakers, and hands-on coding events. Our hope is to inspire the next generation of coders, support both beginners and experienced developers, and create lasting connections in the world of computer science.
